Settings


Wallpaper Method

1. Do [Windows Key] + [R] then type in %appdata%

2. Double-click on the Microsoft folder then double-click on the Windows folder

3. Double-click on the Themes folder and drag your image into the folder.

4. Rename the file to [TranscodedWallpaper] and remove the .png, .jpg, etc.

5. Copy the transcoded wallpaper and drag it to CachedFiles.

6. Copy the name of the CachedImage and delete it.

7. Rename the file that you are trying to set as your wallpaper as the things that you just copied. Example: CachedImage_1280_1024_POS4.

8. Go back to your desktop and right click then press Refresh.

9. That's it! If you're confused then fill out the form on the issues page.
